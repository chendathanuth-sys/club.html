About Our Project

The Challenge

In modern times, many students want to learn more about their specific subjects when their lessons are over, but they face many difficulties such as lack of resources, lack of motivation, and lack of interactivity.

Our Solution

To solve these problems, we have created an interactive Math Quiz game that helps students to learn and practice math in a fun and engaging way.It helps them to improve their math skills and learn their limits while enjoying the process.
